Swiss Chard Burrito with mushrooms and cauliflower

Ingredients
4 tbsp olive oil divided
2 cups shitake mushrooms thinly sliced
1 small yellow onion, peeled, sliced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tbsp ginger, peeled, minced
2 cups cauliflower, riced
8 large swiss chard leaves (stems removed, reserved and chopped into small pieces)
1 15oz can red kidney beans (drained, rinsed)
Kosher salt and freshly ground pepper (to taste)
hot sauce (to serve)
